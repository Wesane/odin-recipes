Odin's Special Coffee

Description

This coffee was made by Odin as he kept falling asleep during Asgard's meetings.
He had asked Hades for help with the coffee beans, the coffee beans were procured
from the deepest depths of the underworld. The sesnation of the coffee has been
described as "frighteningly strong"

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine warm water, instant coffee, and sugar in a sealable jar. Seal and shake
    until foamy.
  3. Pour into a glass full of ice; add milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.
